My last day at BuildDirect as a Co-op

December 15, 2017 / by Andrew Bui

What a great journey!

So eventually, my Co-op work term at BuildDirect has come to an end.

It's been great for the last 8 months being part of the Gateway Supply Chain team. I have met many great people and grown up not only as an engineer/developer but also as a person. I would say that I enjoyed so much working here at BuildDirect; we have built an amazing team with great culture. I am thankful for all the opportunities to develop and contribute myself to the company. I was encouraged to take ownership of the software designs and features which were deployed and used immediately by the clients. I was allowed to pursue my passions and explore the world of modern technologies. Most importantly, out of all the beautiful things, I have made new friends and connected with many brilliant people.

I would like to send my appreciation & gratitude to four of my official ex-supervisors (Anthony, Charles, Jude and Mehdi) as well as the whole Gateway team members that made my time at BuildDirect an unforgettable memory.

Thank you and see you guys soon!
